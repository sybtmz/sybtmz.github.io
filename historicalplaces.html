Lüleburgaz Sokollu Mehmet Paşa Külliyesi

This complex was made by Mimar Sinan on behalf of Sokollu Mehmet Pasha, the great statesman who had been the grand vizier of the Ottoman Empire during the reigns of Sultan Süleyman the Magnificent, Selim II and Murat III.


The construction was completed in 1569-1570.
